About

About my work

What I can do, and my training.

My goals are simple, to fulfill the client's vision; a great design that is simple and clean.
Effective design is when the best technology is seamlessly integrated with the user's experience.
I work directly with clients, brainstorming, prototyping, visualizing, and collaborating in all aspects of developing a project. I've been in the industry for over 20 years, working with a network of seasoned developers, marketing experts to guarantee results on every facet of development.

My expertise includes working on full-stack projects with ReactJS, React Routers and Hooks, Python, PHP, MySQL, and NoSQL-oriented Databases.
Additionally, I am experienced using other JavaScript Frameworks such as Express, VUE, Mongoose, and NodeJS on different servers, mainly Apache. Working with API Libs such as Google's, Rapid, Firebase. Designing with CSS libraries such as BootStrap, WordPress, Sass, Semantics UI, Materials UI
As a designer, I am a certified Adobe CC Instructor InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, DreamWeaver, XD for UI Design and custom elements, and more.
